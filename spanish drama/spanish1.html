Beginning of Spanish Drama

Ernesto

Esmeralda

Lorenco

The sun dipped low over the horizon, casting a golden glow upon the sprawling grounds of the grand mansion in Valencia, Spain. Ernesto stood at the entrance, his hazel eyes surveying the opulent surroundings with a mixture of awe and longing. His muscles rippled beneath the fabric of his unbuttoned shirt, a testament to his strength and determination. Beside him, Esmeralda emerged from the shadows, her slender figure illuminated by the fading light. Her green eyes sparkled with mischief as she took in the sight of the magnificent estate. "Welcome to my humble abode," Ernesto said with a grin, gesturing towards the mansion with a flourish. "I hope you find it to your liking." Esmeralda laughed, a melodic sound that echoed through the grand foyer. "It's more than I ever imagined," she admitted, her gaze lingering on Ernesto's rugged features. "But it's not the mansion that captivates me." Ernesto's heart skipped a beat as he met Esmeralda's gaze, his chest swelling with a sense of pride. "Then what is it, my dear?" he asked, his voice tinged with anticipation. Before Esmeralda could respond, a booming voice echoed from the depths of the mansion, interrupting their conversation. "Ernesto, my old friend! Is that you?" Turning towards the sound, Ernesto's eyes widened in surprise as Lorenco emerged from the shadows, his towering frame filling the room with an undeniable presence. Bald-headed and adorned with tattoos, Lorenco cut a striking figure as he strode towards them, his croptop and Balenciaga shoes adding to his eccentric charm. "Lorenco!" Ernesto exclaimed, a grin spreading across his face. "It's been too long, my friend. What brings you to Valencia?" Lorenco chuckled, clapping Ernesto on the shoulder with a force that nearly knocked him off balance. "I could ask you the same question," he replied, his blue eyes twinkling with mischief. "But I think I already know the answer." Esmeralda watched the exchange with interest, her curiosity piqued by the undeniable chemistry between the two men. Sensing her gaze, Lorenco turned towards her, his eyebrow raised inquisitively. "And who might this lovely creature be?" he asked, his voice dripping with charm. Ernesto's heart clenched at the sight of Lorenco's flirtatious grin, a pang of jealousy coursing through him. "This is Esmeralda," he said, forcing a smile. "She's a dear friend of mine." Esmeralda offered Lorenco a coy smile, her green eyes dancing with amusement. "It's a pleasure to meet you, Lorenco," she said, extending her hand. Lorenco took her hand in his own, his touch sending a shiver down her spine. "The pleasure is all mine, my dear," he replied, pressing a kiss to the back of her hand. As they exchanged pleasantries, Ernesto felt a knot form in the pit of his stomach, a sense of unease settling over him like a dark cloud. Little did he know, the arrival of Lorenco would set into motion a chain of events that would change their lives forever.
